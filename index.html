About

This page accompanies our survey/paper on visual puzzles for probing reasoning in Large Vision-Language Models. We organize puzzle types, discuss evaluation settings, and highlight the gap between real reasoning and pattern matching.

Why visual puzzles?

Controlled reasoning tests

Visual puzzles isolate compositionality, abstraction, analogy, and rule induction.

Stress-testing LVLMs

They reveal where strong performance may come from shortcuts rather than reasoning.

Benchmarking progress

They offer a compact and interpretable way to compare models and prompting methods.
